This is the image used for the ship the player has to get on at the end of the protectorate introduction/tutorial mission.
The best thing to do is to take your ship tier 3 non lit image and put the blocks in manually, or just cut them out of the included human ship place holder.

When renaming files please make sure that the name of the files follow these templates:
Ship Image: protectorateship<YourRaceId>.png

Where:
  <YourRaceId> is the "kind" value you assigned to your species in your .species file.
