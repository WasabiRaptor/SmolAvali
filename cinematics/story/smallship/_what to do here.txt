When renaming files please make sure that the name of the files follow these templates:
  frames: <YourRaceId>aiportrait.frames
portrait: <YourRaceId>questportrait.png
  static: static<YourRaceId>.png

Where:
  <YourRaceId> is the "kind" value you assigned to your species in your .species file,
    in all of the cases here, the value race name must start with a capital letter, for example:
    SmolAvaliAI.png, SmolAvaliAI.frames, staticSmolAvali.png
